How To Read This Dashboard

The thesis: AI's real story isn't NVIDIA's stock price — it's the entire supply chain underneath it. This dashboard tracks 8 layers from atoms to APIs.

Pipeline nodes show each layer's current health. Green = broadly up. Red = pressure. Amber = mixed signals.

Click any layer to deep-dive into its watchlist, key metrics, and supply chain context.

Custom tickers: On each layer page, add your own tickers to any category. They persist in your browser across sessions via localStorage.

Top movers surface the biggest moves across ALL layers, including your custom additions.
